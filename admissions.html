Requirements

Applicant must:

be a bonafide resident of Quezon Province, Lucena City and Tayabas City;
be an incoming Grade 7 Student;
be physically and psychology fit to undergo rigorous academic program;
have a good aptitude in Science, Mathematics and English;
have a rating of 85% or above in Science, Mathematics and English during the 1st and 2nd Quarters of the current school year;
have a final rating of 83% and above in all other subjects;
have a general average of 85% and above
have not taken the Quezon Science High School Admission Test (QSHSAT) previously
For an applicant who had already completed Grade 6, the student must satisfy all of the above requirements and have not yet enrolled in Grade 7.

Requirements

Things to bring / required:

Application Form
Photocopy of Form 138
Photocopy of NSO or PSA Birth Certificate
Certifacte of Good Moral (signed by the Principal)
All requirements should be submitted on or before January 5, 2018 at
QSHS Admission Secretariat, Isabang, Tayabas City.
Incomplete documents will not be processed.
There will be a testing fee of Php 50.00 to cover the expenses related to the admission test.
