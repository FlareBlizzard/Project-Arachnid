FAQs

FAQ1: Libre po ba ang lahat?

Yes, pero madaming miscellaneous (e.g. projects, ambangan)

FAQ2: May intramurals po ba kayo?

Yes we have, although sports are not the school's priority.

FAQ3: Ano - ano po ang mga subjects niyo?

For Junior High School, may eight (8) core subjects and three (3) elective subjects:

English
Filipino
Mathematics
Araling Panlipunan
Science
TLE / ICT
Music, Arts, Physical Education and Health (MAPEH)
Edukasyon sa Pagpapakatao

For Senior High School:

FAQ4: Ano po ang passing grade para ma-maintain ang iyong scholarship?

Wala, kasi God loves us.

FAQ5: Pwede po ba magtransfer from other schools to QSHS?

Pwede, pero dapat galing sa ibang science high school

FAQ6: Pwede po ba mag Senior High School (SHS) sa QSHS kahit nag-Junior High School sa iba?

Nope

FAQ7: Anong mga strands ang ino-offer ng QSHS for Senior High School?

Our school only offers STEM track

FAQ8: Sino lang po pwede mag-dorm?

Open for all students, pero mas prioritized ang mga nakatira sa malalayong bayan.

FAQ9: May free internet po ba sa QSHS?

No, we prefer our students to provide their own source.

FAQ10: May free tablets po ba kayo?

Meron, pero

FAQ11: Pwede po bang bumisita ang mga magulang sa dorm?

Yes, pero may allowed time lang.

FAQ12: Madalas ba ang activities sa school nyo?

Yes, there are times na madaming events due to multple club activities na nagkakasabay sabay

FAQ13: May clubs po ba sa school nyo?

Meron, in fact we have 12 established organizations. Learn more here

FAQ14: Kailangan po ba na magaling sa Science and/or Math para maging honor student?

Yes, because we always aim for academic excellence especially in the field of Science, Mathematics and Technology
